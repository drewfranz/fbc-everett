Welcome!

First Baptist Church is a diverse community of believers GROWING TOGETHER IN CHRIST! We are passionate about God's vision for us of growing together in our commitment to Christ, growing together in our connection to one another in the body of Christ and growing together in our contribution to the mission of Christ.

For us, the spiritual life can be summed up in one word: Relationships! Life's most important relationship is one God offers to us freely through personal faith in Jesus.
